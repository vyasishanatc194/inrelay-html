Frequently asked questions

Q

Do you have a setup cost?

A

Absolutely not. All plans are month to month with no contracts, no setup fees, and no hidden gimmicks. Cancel anytime.

Q

Do you offer yearly plans?

A

If you pay for a year upfront (optional) you are eligible for a 10% discount.

Q

What payment methods do you accept?

A

Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. For annual plans we can also accept checks and wire transfers.

Q

Do you have any dealer group discounts?

A

Yes! We offer dealer group discounts on annual plans for more than 4 dealerships. Please contact us if you are buying a plan for more than 4 dealerships.
